Use Cases

As a supervisor I can connect myself to the ILVO system, I can then go in and create a session in order to build an availability sheet for the lifeguards to fill out. Once the lifeguards have filled out these availability sheets I will then be able to see them and apply them to the schedule that is available on the shifts present in the diagram of the shift management tab. Once those have been created the report of which shifts are taking place one can be sent to administration in order for them to manage that schedule and send the employees their personalized schedules.

As a lifeguard I can connect to the ILVO system and put in my availabilities for the upcoming season of lifeguarding. Instead of having to take a document and submit it by email instead connect to the online system and directly give my availabilities through the customized availabilities form that my supervisor created once I clicked confirm the supervisor can directly have access to this in order to schedule me for shifts in the upcoming future.



Challenges and Features

  • Database created by parsing existing excel documentation converted to CSV format
  • Handle multiple sessions simultaneously across many different activities (free swimming, teaching, lifeguarding classes etc)
  • Take into account a legal document outlining the procedures that had to persist after the switch to a web app from a manual system
  • Turned the multi-day task of assigning a new semester schedule to a 30 minute task
  • Allowed CRUD operations on the database by authorized users to ensure old excel documents wouldn't need to be kept up to date
  • Systems creates login for every employee and supervisor featuring secure encryption
